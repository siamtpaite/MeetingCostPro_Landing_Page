MeetingCost PRO Extension

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

MeetingCost PRO ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Email address – Used for account identification and login
  • Username (USER ID) – Optional display name you choose
  • Password – Encrypted and stored securely via Firebase Authentication

1.2 Meeting Data

  • Transcripts – Audio transcriptions from meetings you record
  • Summaries – AI-generated meeting summaries
  • Participant lists – Names and roles of meeting attendees you add
  • Meeting duration and cost – Calculated based on participants and time

1.3 Usage Data

  • Feature usage statistics
  • AI provider preferences (AssemblyAI, Deepgram, OpenAI Whisper, Google Cloud, HuggingFace, Rev.ai)
  • Recording settings and preferences

1.4 Payment Information

  • License keys – For PRO account verification
  • Purchase confirmations – Processed by Gumroad or Plisio (we do not store credit card details)

2. How and Where We Store Your Data

2.1 Local Storage (Your Browser)

  • Participant lists – Stored locally in Chrome storage
  • Extension settings – Overlay preferences, currency, working hours
  • Templates – Saved meeting templates
  • Session state – Temporary meeting data

2.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)

  • Account information – Email, username, PRO license status
  • Trial usage – Free trial consumption tracking
  • Meeting history – Past meeting costs and summaries (if synced)

2.3 AI Provider Servers (Your Choice)

When you use your own API key for transcription, audio and transcripts are sent to the AI provider you select. We do not store your transcripts unless you explicitly sync them to Firestore. Each provider has its own data retention policy:

  • AssemblyAI – Retains transcripts for 7 days by default
  • Deepgram – Retains audio for 24 hours (configurable)
  • OpenAI Whisper – Does not retain audio or transcripts
  • Google Cloud STT – Retains audio only during processing
  • HuggingFace – Inference only, no storage
  • Rev.ai – Retains transcripts for 30 days

3. Data Retention

  • Account data (Firestore) – Stored as long as your account is active. Deletion can be requested at any time.
  • Local data (Chrome storage) – Remains until you uninstall the extension or clear browser data.
  • Meeting transcripts – Stored locally by default. Only synced to cloud if you enable cloud backup.

4. Third-Party Services

MeetingCost PRO integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage
  • Google Cloud Speech-to-Text: Audio transcription (when selected)
  • AssemblyAI / Deepgram / OpenAI Whisper / HuggingFace / Rev.ai: AI transcription providers you may choose
  • Gumroad / Plisio: Payment processing for PRO licenses
  • Vercel: Hosting for backend services

Each service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Privacy & Compliance Controls (User Settings)

The extension provides several privacy controls within the PRO Dashboard:

5.1 Auto-Delete Audio Chunks

Audio recordings sent to AI providers can be automatically deleted based on your preference:

  • Immediately after transcription
  • After 24 hours
  • After 7 days
  • After 30 days
  • Never (manual cleanup only)

5.2 Auto-Delete Transcripts

Transcripts can be automatically deleted from local storage based on your preference:

  • Never
  • After 30 days
  • After 90 days
  • After 1 year

5.3 Content Security

  • Redact PII in transcripts: Automatically masks names, emails, and phone numbers
  • Prefer Local Processing: Use local models if available (requires high-spec PC)

5.4 Compliance Notifications

  • Show recording disclaimer: Alerts meeting participants that AI notes are being taken
  • Ask for host consent: Requires manual approval before starting any recording
Note: These settings are user-controlled. MeetingCost PRO does not enforce or override your privacy preferences.

6. Data Deletion Instructions

You can delete your account and associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Email Request (Available Now)

Send an email to siam_t_paite@icloud.com with:

  • Subject: "DELETE MY ACCOUNT"
  • Your registered email address
  • Confirmation that you want all data permanently removed

We will process deletion requests within 14 days.

Option 2: Through the Extension (Coming Soon)

  1. Open the MeetingCost PRO extension popup
  2. Go to PRO Dashboard
  3. Click "Delete Account" (feature in development)

Option 2: Delete Account Button (PRO Dashboard)

PRO users can delete their account directly from the extension:

  1. Open MeetingCost PRO popup
  2. Click "PRO Dashboard"
  3. Click "Delete Account & All Data"
  4. Confirm permanent deletion

This removes your account from our servers and clears all local data immediately.

7. Security

  • All data transmitted between the extension and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS
  • Passwords are hashed and stored securely via Firebase Authentication
  • API keys are stored in Chrome's secure session storage, never in source code
  • Access to production databases is restricted to authorized personnel only

8. Children's Privacy

MeetingCost PRO is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes via the extension or by email. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to certain data processing
  • Export your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at siam_t_paite@icloud.com.
